About this website

A simple interactive hub built to present and explore audio content.

AI Podcast Hub is designed as a polished browser-based experience where users can browse curated podcasts, open episode details, search by tags, add their own audio, and use a built-in AI page to generate podcast-style scripts instantly. The layout is structured to feel modern, fast, and easy to navigate while still keeping the content focused and visually engaging.
